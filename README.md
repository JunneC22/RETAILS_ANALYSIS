# RETAILS_ANALYSIS

# 📊 Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (SQL + Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard built using SQL and Power BI. The goal is to analyze transactional retail data to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, product performance, and customer segmentation.

The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions by providing a clear and interactive view of business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance using KPIs
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Segment customers based on spending value
- Visualize trends over time

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies
- SQL (Data extraction and preparation)
- Microsoft Power BI (Data visualization)
- DAX (KPI calculations and segmentation)
- Excel / CSV dataset

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Order Value (AOV)

---

## 🏗️ Dashboard Structure

### 📌 Page 1: Executive Overview
- KPI Cards (Revenue, Transactions, AOV)
- Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
- Revenue by Product Category (Bar Chart)

### 📌 Page 2: Product Performance
- Revenue by Product Category
- Units Sold per Category
- Product performance comparison

### 📌 Page 3: Customer Analysis
- Top 10 Customers
- Customer Segmentation (High / Medium / Low Value)
- Customer distribution insights

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue shows variation over time, indicating possible seasonal trends.
- A small number of product categories contribute most of the revenue.
- Customer segmentation shows that a small group of high-value customers contributes significantly to total revenue.
- Most customers fall into the low-value segment, indicating a long-tail distribution.

## 📈 Business Impact
